Guide & rules
Purpose
Track all CS Technologies contacts across past clients and cold targets. Manage touch frequency to stay visible without aggressive outreach.
Relationship types
Past Client — Someone from a company CS Tech has previously worked with. Highest priority.\n\nCold Target — No prior relationship. Do not contact directly yet. Build visibility via content/LinkedIn first.\n\nWarm Lead — Cold contact that has engaged. Can move to light direct outreach.
Status flags (auto-calculated)
Warm — Last touch within 30 days.\nOverdue — Past the next touch date.\nCold — Never been touched.\nPassive — Touched before, but not recently overdue.
Touch cadence
Cadence is set per priority tier in Settings. High = 3 months by default, Mid = 4, Low = 6. Change these at any time — all next-touch dates recalculate automatically.
Important notes
Sign-off — All outreach must be reviewed by a senior team member.\nUpdates — After every touch, log the interaction so the tracker stays current.\nNew contacts — Fill all columns before marking active.
