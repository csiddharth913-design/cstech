Guide & rules
Purpose
Track all CS Technologies contacts across past clients and cold targets. Manage touch frequency to stay visible without aggressive outreach.
Status flags (auto-calculated)
Warm — Last touch within 30 days. Overdue — Past the next touch date. Cold — Never been touched. Passive — Touched before, but not recently overdue.
Touch cadence
Cadence is set per priority tier in Settings. High = 3 months by default, Mid = 4, Low = 6. All next-touch dates recalculate automatically when you change these.
Important notes
Sign-off — All outreach must be reviewed by a senior team member. Updates — After every touch, log the interaction to keep the tracker current. New contacts — Fill all columns before marking active.
